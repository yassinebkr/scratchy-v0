⚡ Critical Notes:
  • IBT-2 R_EN + L_EN → tied to 3.3V (always enabled). Saves 4 GPIO.
  • Encoder power → 3.3V from ESP32 (encoder supports 3.3V/5V)
  • PSU → 12V, minimum 5A (handles 2× 11A stall with headroom)
  • Decoupling → 100µF electrolytic near each IBT-2 B+, 100nF ceramic near each encoder VCC
  • BTS7960 IS pin → 8500:1 ratio ≈ 13mV at 11A stall (too weak for ESP32 ADC). Use ACS712-20A (€1-2) for reliable sensing, or software-limit PWM to 60-70%.
  • All GNDs must be common — ESP32, IBT-2s, PSU, encoders on one bus.
  • Encoder resolution → 16 PPR × 4 (quadrature) × gear ratio = counts/rev
  • IBT-2 control → Forward: RPWM=PWM, LPWM=LOW · Reverse: RPWM=LOW, LPWM=PWM · Brake: both HIGH
